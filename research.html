Research

Research Focus

Applied linguistics, language education, second language acquisition, and AI-assisted language learning.

Research Areas

Core Interests

Second Language Acquisition

Research interests include how learners develop grammar, pronunciation, and metalinguistic awareness across different stages of second language learning.

Applied Linguistics

Applied linguistics provides the main framework for connecting language theory with teaching, learning, assessment, and multilingual communication.

AI-assisted Language Learning

How artificial intelligence can support feedback, pronunciation, and learner autonomy in language education.

French as a Foreign Language / FLE

FLE research focuses on French language learning, grammar acquisition, learner difficulties, and pedagogical approaches for French as an additional language.

Language Policy and Language Education

How language policy shapes educational practice, multilingual learning environments, and access to language education.
