About the project

Experimental pronunciation support

EuuuhFrench is an experimental language learning tool by Sifaks Ousmail. It explores how AI-assisted feedback, applied linguistics, and language education can support Chinese learners of French pronunciation.

Learning support

What it aims to help with

The prototype focuses on careful, research-informed pronunciation guidance rather than a public product launch.

French pronunciation practice

Focused practice for sounds, rhythm, and pronunciation patterns in French.

AI-assisted feedback

Exploring how automated feedback can guide learners without replacing teaching or research judgment.

Chinese learners of French

Designed around challenges that can appear when Chinese-speaking learners work on French pronunciation.

Applied linguistics and language education

Connected to language learning research, pronunciation pedagogy, and multilingual education.
